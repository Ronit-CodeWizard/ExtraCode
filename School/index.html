A Premier Educational Institution

Atomic Energy Central School, Narwapahar is a distinguished educational institution located in the peaceful campus of Uranium Corporation of India Limited (UCIL) township. Situated on the scenic banks of river Gurrah, our school provides an ideal learning environment away from urban chaos.

Under the aegis of Atomic Energy Education Society, we are committed to providing quality education that combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. Our focus extends beyond academics to nurture well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

ADMISSIONS

Join Our Family

Begin your child's journey towards academic excellence and holistic development

1

Obtain Application

Download the application form from our website or collect it from the school office during working hours. Forms are available during the admission period.

2

Submit Documents

Complete the application form and submit it along with required documents including birth certificate, transfer certificate, photographs, and previous academic records.

3

Entrance Test

Appear for the entrance examination and interaction session on the scheduled date. Parents and guardians are required to accompany the student.

4

Confirmation

Upon selection, complete the admission formalities within the stipulated time and pay the admission and tuition fees to confirm the seat.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Class I: Minimum age of 6 years as on 31st March of the admission year
  • Classes II-IX: Based on seat availability and qualifying the entrance assessment
  • Class XI: Minimum 60% in Class X board examination with required subject combinations
  • Transfer Cases: Valid transfer certificate from previous school and satisfactory academic record
